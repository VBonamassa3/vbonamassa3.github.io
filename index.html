About Me

I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science who is passionate about building clean, well-structured software and creating user experiences that are both functional and visually polished. My approach to development emphasizes readability, maintainability, and thoughtful design. I enjoy taking projects from concept to completion while ensuring the final product is intuitive, efficient, and aesthetically refined. Currently, I am continuing to expand my technical skill set with a strong focus on Java, while also developing experience in Android application development using Kotlin. In addition, I work with web technologies including JavaScript and CSS to build responsive, well-designed interfaces. Beyond software, I have a deep passion for PC building and general computer support. This hands-on experience with hardware architecture and troubleshooting has sharpened my problem-solving skills and given me a holistic understanding of how software interacts with physical systems. As an early-career developer, I am eager to contribute to a collaborative team where I can continue to grow as a programmer, solve meaningful problems, and deliver high-quality software solutions.
